# Projectile-Interception-system

this project is a very simple air defense system for us to play with.

it has three interceptors of different velocities and on gives two sets of points that are one second apart.

it finds projectiles speed, checks which is the best interceptor for interception.

and at what angles should it be launched and at what time it hits.

The projectile cannot be intercepted with a speed of 80
The projectile can be intercepted with a speed of 150 at time 2 seconds, with an azimuth of 0 degrees and an elevation of 90 degrees
The projectile can be intercepted with a speed of 250 at time 0.6666666666666666 seconds, with an azimuth of 0 degrees and an elevation of 90 degrees
